CMOS :

          The full form of CMOS is Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor. 
          CMOS is an integrated circuit built on a printed circuit board. It is a 
          battery-powered memory chip that effortlessly holds the initialisation 
          data. The BIOS uses this data to turn on the device, i.e., 
          during the bootup process.
